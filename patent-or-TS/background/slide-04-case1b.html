Case 1B: Customer List Protection

PharmaSales Corp

The Situation

  • Database of 500 hospital purchasing managers built over 3 years
  • Includes names, contact info, purchasing preferences, budget cycles
  • Sales representative left to join competitor and took customer list
  • Some information publicly available in RFP processes and annual reports
  • Company had confidentiality agreements with sales reps

Could the company have patented this customer list? Can they sue for trade secret misappropriation?
