Case 4A: Biotech Therapeutic Platform

BioTherapeutics Inc.

Technology Stack Components

  • Target Discovery Algorithm: ML algorithm for identifying therapeutic targets (cloud SaaS)
  • Antibody Design Method: Computational modeling for designing antibody sequences
  • Proprietary Cell Line: Engineered CHO cell line with higher protein yields
  • Bioreactor Control System: Specialized hardware/software for fermentation control
  • Purification Process: Multi-step chromatography achieving 99.9 percent purity
  • Quality Control Assays: Novel assays faster and more sensitive than industry standard
  • Platform Brand: BioExpress trademark associated with quality and reliability

For each component, determine the optimal IP protection strategy. Consider patents, trade secrets, copyright, trademark, and hybrid approaches.
