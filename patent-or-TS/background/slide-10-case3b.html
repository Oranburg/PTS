Case 3B: Search Algorithm

Search Engine Innovations (Late 1990s-Present)

The Situation

  • Novel algorithm for ranking web search results (PageRank) developed in late 1990s
  • Company initially patented core PageRank algorithm (expired 2018)
  • Hundreds of additional ranking factors kept as trade secrets
  • Algorithm updated constantly (500+ changes per year)
  • Dominant market position (90 percent market share)
  • Fast-moving industry requiring constant innovation
  • Revealing ranking factors would enable manipulation by SEO practitioners

Why use a hybrid approach (patent + trade secret)? What are the strategic considerations?
