Case 3C: Pharma Intermediate Process

ChemSynth Pharma

The Situation

  • Novel 7-step synthesis process for key intermediate in blockbuster drug ($2B revenue annually)
  • Final drug molecule already patented (expires in 8 years)
  • Process reduces manufacturing costs by 40 percent vs. alternative routes
  • FDA requires manufacturing process disclosure but not all optimization details
  • Generic manufacturers will enter after drug patent expiration
  • Some process details observable from impurity profiles in final product
  • Process patent would expire 20 years from filing (possibly after drug patent)

Patent the process, keep it as a trade secret, or use a hybrid strategy? Analyze the tradeoffs.
